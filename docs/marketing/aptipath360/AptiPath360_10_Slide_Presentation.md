# AptiPath360 10-Slide Presentation

## Slide 1: Title
**AptiPath360: Career Clarity for Grade 8 to Post-12**  
Subtext: Structured career discovery for students, parents, and institutions.

Speaker note: Introduce AptiPath360 as a decision-quality product, not just a test.

## Slide 2: The Core Problem
- Students choose streams without enough clarity
- Parents have anxiety and conflicting advice
- Institutions struggle to offer consistent guidance quality

Speaker note: Frame this as a decision system gap.

## Slide 3: Product Overview
- Parent context + student assessment
- Multi-dimensional diagnostic model
- Actionable output with next steps

Speaker note: Explain end-to-end flow in 30 seconds.

## Slide 4: 7 Diagnostic Dimensions
- Core Aptitude
- Applied Challenge
- Interest and Work Preference
- Values and Motivation
- Learning Behavior
- AI Readiness
- Career Reality Check

Speaker note: Show this is broader than classic aptitude tests.

## Slide 5: Report Output
- Top career-fit recommendations
- Stream-fit insights
- Strength and risk indicators
- 90-day action plan

Speaker note: Emphasize practical usability.

## Slide 6: Why Institutions Adopt
- Standardized counseling framework
- Better parent trust and transparency
- Early mismatch correction
- Easy pilot-to-scale rollout

Speaker note: Tie product value to institution outcomes.

## Slide 7: Use Cases
- School career cells
- Coaching institutes
- Parent-first direct advisory model

Speaker note: Mention flexible implementation models.

## Slide 8: Commercial Models
- Per-student pricing
- Batch package pricing
- Pilot bundle for first cohort

Speaker note: Offer pilot-first entry for easier approvals.

## Slide 9: Implementation Timeline
- Week 1: Setup and orientation
- Week 2: Assessment execution
- Week 3: Report delivery
- Week 4: Review and refinement

Speaker note: Keep rollout simple and predictable.

## Slide 10: Closing CTA
**Start with a pilot batch and measure outcomes in 2 to 4 weeks.**

Speaker note: Ask for pilot size, target grade, and preferred start date.
