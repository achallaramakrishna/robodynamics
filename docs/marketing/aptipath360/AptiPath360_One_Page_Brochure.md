p# AptiPath360
## Career Discovery and Decision Support for Grade 8 to Post-12

### Problem

Students and parents are confused about:
- Which stream and career to choose
- Whether a chosen path matches actual aptitude and motivation
- How to convert guidance into action

Most career guidance is generic, one-time, and not actionable.

### Solution

AptiPath360 is a structured, adaptive career discovery system that combines:
- Parent intake insights
- Student diagnostic assessment
- AI-assisted analysis with practical interpretation

### What AptiPath360 delivers

- Top career-fit recommendations
- Stream-fit direction (for higher classes)
- Strength map across key dimensions
- Risk flags and mismatch indicators
- 90-day action plan for student and parent

### Assessment coverage

The student journey evaluates:
1. Core Aptitude
2. Applied Challenge
3. Interest and Work Preference
4. Values and Motivation
5. Learning Behavior
6. AI Readiness
7. Career Reality Check

### Who it is for

- Schools (career guidance cell)
- Coaching institutes
- Parents of Grade 8 to Post-12 students
- Student counseling and mentoring centers

### Why clients buy AptiPath360

- Clear and structured career decisions
- Better parent-student alignment
- Early correction of wrong stream assumptions
- Actionable report instead of generic advice
- Scalable process for institutions

### Engagement model (sample)

- Individual student report
- School/coaching batch assessment
- Parent briefing and counselor support
