# AptiPath360 Sales Deck Script

Use this as spoken script and slide content.

## Slide 1: Title

**AptiPath360: Career Clarity for Grade 8 to Post-12**

Speaker note:
"Today we will show how AptiPath360 helps schools and families make better career decisions with structure, evidence, and action."

## Slide 2: The current gap

- Students choose based on trend, peer pressure, or incomplete information
- Parents are unsure how to evaluate real fit
- Counseling is often one-time and generic

Speaker note:
"The issue is not lack of advice. The issue is lack of decision quality."

## Slide 3: What AptiPath360 is

- Parent + student integrated journey
- Adaptive diagnostic flow
- Practical report with next-step plan

Speaker note:
"AptiPath360 is not just a quiz. It is a guided career decision system."

## Slide 4: What gets evaluated

1. Core Aptitude
2. Applied Challenge
3. Interest and Work Preference
4. Values and Motivation
5. Learning Behavior
6. AI Readiness
7. Career Reality Check

## Slide 5: What report includes

- Top career-fit recommendations
- Stream-fit signals
- Strength and gap indicators
- Parent-aligned action points
- 90-day roadmap

## Slide 6: Institutional value

- Better quality counseling conversations
- Structured evidence for guidance decisions
- Improves trust with parents
- Creates measurable career-guidance process

## Slide 7: Delivery model

- Individual assessment
- Class-wise or batch-wise rollout
- Parent briefing support
- Optional counselor enablement

## Slide 8: Commercial packaging (customizable)

- Per-student pricing
- Bundle for schools/coaching centers
- Pilot package for first cohort

Speaker note:
"We can start with a pilot batch and scale after outcome review."

## Slide 9: Implementation timeline

- Week 1: setup and orientation
- Week 2: assessment window
- Week 3: report delivery
- Week 4: parent/counselor review

## Slide 10: Closing

"If you want, we can start with a pilot for your selected class and show outcomes in 2 to 4 weeks."
