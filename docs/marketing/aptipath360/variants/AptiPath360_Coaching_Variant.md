# AptiPath360 Sales Variant: Coaching Institute

## Positioning

AptiPath360 helps coaching institutes improve student path clarity, reduce wrong-track preparation, and strengthen parent trust.

## Primary Buyer

- Center Director
- Academic Head
- Counseling Team Lead

## Core Value for Coaching

- Better stream and program alignment before intensive prep
- Early detection of student mismatch risk
- Stronger parent communication with structured reports
- More focused student goal planning

## Suggested Offer

- Pilot for one batch (example: Grade 10 or 11)
- Student diagnostic + parent alignment report
- Follow-up review call for top risk/mismatch cases

## Decision Triggers

- Low consistency in student motivation
- Parents questioning coaching-path suitability
- High variance in student fit vs chosen target exams

## Closing Line

"Run AptiPath360 before deep prep cycles so students invest effort in the right direction."
