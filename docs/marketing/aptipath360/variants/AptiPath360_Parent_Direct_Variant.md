# AptiPath360 Sales Variant: Parent Direct

## Positioning

AptiPath360 gives parents a structured way to make confident career and stream decisions with their child.

## Primary Buyer

- Parent of Grade 8 to Post-12 student

## Core Value for Parents

- Reduces confusion and fear of wrong decisions
- Converts discussion into structured action plan
- Gives practical report, not generic advice
- Improves parent-student alignment on next steps

## Suggested Offer

- Single student diagnostic package
- Report walkthrough call
- 90-day implementation tracker for parent and student

## Decision Triggers

- Stream selection confusion
- Student motivation inconsistency
- Parent-child disagreement on career direction
- Concern about future relevance and AI-era careers

## Closing Line

"If you want clarity with action, AptiPath360 is the fastest way to move from confusion to a practical plan."
