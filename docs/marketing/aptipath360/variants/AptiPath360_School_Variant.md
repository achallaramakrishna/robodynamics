# AptiPath360 Sales Variant: School

## Positioning

AptiPath360 helps schools deliver structured, evidence-backed career guidance at scale for Grade 8 to Post-12 students.

## Primary Buyer

- Principal
- Academic Director
- Career Guidance Coordinator

## Core Value for Schools

- Standardized counseling process across classes
- Parent communication confidence with clear reports
- Better alignment between student profile and stream planning
- Institutional credibility in career guidance outcomes

## Suggested Offer

- Pilot with 1 to 2 grades
- Parent orientation webinar included
- Individual reports + summary insights for school leadership

## Decision Triggers

- Demand from parents for stronger career guidance
- Need for structured process before stream selection
- Need to differentiate school outcomes

## Closing Line

"Start with one pilot cohort and we will show measurable improvement in counseling quality and parent confidence."
